My name is Lydia. I'm an instructional designer at Colorado State University Online. My job is to build online courses. I work with faculty to develop learning objectives, and then match a curriculum that we can assess at the end to make sure that students are actually learning what we want them to learn. I come in at every step of the process to develop learning materials. I spend a lot of my time shooting, editing and producing video, doing audio cleanup, writing code, and building e-learning games to make sure that distance education is engaging and most of all effective.
